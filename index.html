About us

Who We Are

Tech4Hood in essence is an organization created to provide our underrepresented youth in minority communities with the skills and tools they need to be successful in today's society driven highly by technology advances in the field of education, medicine, transportation and an opportunity to bring them up to speed and encourage them to pursue careers in each of these fields, and bring these opportunities much closer to them, in collaboration with the local educational institutions in order to further their education by pushing the limits of what their surroundings allow them.

As a result, it will open the doors to new employment opportunities, provide high-wage jobs related to science and technology and will provide them with entrepreneurial skills required to compete in a hi-tech marketplace with the goal of making a difference in their communities and demolish the barrier of inequality in today's workforce.

What We Do

Our ultimate goal is to penetrate our minority communities with underrepresented youth with the goal of demystifying learning computer science and technology-related courses, bringing closer to them the necessary educational tools and opportunities which will put them on the right path and in the long run will provide them with rewarding careers, a better and brighter future and as a consequence high-paying jobs.

At the same time mobilize our youth and prepare them for the next technological wave that's about to come, which will impact all aspects of our society, and we need to help unlock that untapped potential within our communities in the current generation as well as establish a solid legacy for future generations to come.
